ELVINA DARCLEUR - PROFESSIONAL PIRATE

Elvina is an elf who hails from the distant land of Tarlklon, but has taken to the seas for the possibility of riches. Also for the joy of sheer bloodlust.

She has a lot of mannerisms, most of them are terrible. She swears a lot.

Her ability is to charm anyone. Leaving them robbed and most likely unconscious.

Elvina is fun to have at parties!
